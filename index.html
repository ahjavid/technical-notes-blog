Multi-GPU Training: Hardware Topology Analysis

Jun 2025

Why more GPUs doesn't always mean better performance. 120+ hours of testing with dual RTX 4070 Ti SUPER reveals critical hardware topology considerations.

  • Models under 10M parameters perform worse on multi-GPU
  • PCIe Host Bridge prevents P2P GPU communication
  • Cost-benefit analysis reveals negative ROI scenarios

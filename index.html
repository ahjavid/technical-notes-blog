Multi-GPU Training Performance: When Hardware Topology Matters

June 17, 2025 25 min read Performance Analysis

What happens when you throw dual RTX 4070 Ti SUPER GPUs at a machine learning training problem? The answer might surprise you. This comprehensive analysis reveals why more GPUs doesn't always mean better performance, and how hardware topology can make or break your training efficiency.

Key Research Findings

  • Hardware topology is critical: PCIe Host Bridge connections create 20-30% communication overhead that can negate multi-GPU benefits
  • Model size threshold discovered: Models under 5M parameters consistently show negative scaling with multi-GPU training
  • Intelligent strategy prevents waste: Model-aware GPU selection automatically chooses optimal configuration
  • Production implications: Current AI workloads may be better served by single GPU optimization
